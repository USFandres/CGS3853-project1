About Us

The Jordan Brand is a subsidiary of Nike, Inc. It was created for and endorsed by basketball legend Michael Jordan. Established in 1997, the brand is renowned for producing high-quality athletic footwear, apparel, and accessories. The Air Jordan line, which includes signature basketball sneakers and lifestyle shoes, is one of the most iconic and influential in the history of sports and fashion. Jordan Brand has also expanded its reach beyond basketball, collaborating with athletes, musicians, and designers to create a wide range of products. It has become a global cultural phenomenon, known for its distinctive Jumpman logo and its impact on both sports and popular culture.
